Challenges

Bank promotions to give discounts on products was a challenge with the existing payment gateway

02

Managing and configuring multiple payment gateways into the system dynamically atruntime was notfeasible

03

Due to variety of payment options and their interdependencies, the existing technology stack had limitations to cater to all the expected enhancements.

04

Reconciliation of different payment gateways individually was a huge overhead

Solution Overview

Hybris Commerce Cloud, SAP Data Services, SAP Digital Interconnect for emails,HCI (Hana Cloud Integration)

Implementation Success

Integration of Shoppers Stop e-commerce with Juspay enabled trendy features which were not possible with the previous payment gateway provider. eg: routing the request to appropriated gateways based on the priority logic;switch the gateways based on the availability of the systems.

Configuration of the payments from Juspay dashboard with minimal changes on the SAP Commerce system.

Implementation of the new payment gateway across all channels- desktop, mobile and tablet

Bank discount promotions and corresponding marketing messages is driven via a dashboard.

Benefits

01

Improved reconciliation time as the reports can be pulled from a single dashboard for all different payment gateways

02
