Adobe Commerce Implementation For Wiltshire Farm Foods

Wiltshire Farm Foods is a brand of apetito which is into preparation and delivery of meals and other food items to both indiv idual customers as well as business entities. The company currently has presence in the UK, Ireland, USA & Canada. This site is planned to go -live in Jan’23 / Feb’23

This Magento based Commerce, Seller portal and Admin capabilities enable WFF to generate orders. 80% of the orders are placed via the Seller portal with assistance from the customer service agents and the remaining 20% of orders are self-serviced by customers eCommerce site.

Wiltshire Farm Foods Client
SAP B2B Project
Zinc & Silver Industry
-- Services

The Customers Challenge

Wiltshire Farm Foods was initially developed on Kentico platform which was having multiple challenges as mentioned below

01

Disjointed processes between Orders placed via MS Dynamics (CSA) vs Orders placed via the website.

02

Redundancy of operations due to disparate systems (Kentico & MS Dynaimcs)

03

Effective management of the content within the site which had a lot of technical dependencies

04

Page Loading issues & site was not Mobile User Friendly

05

Performance issues with Kentico CMS admin

Solution Overview

Reduce the operations overheads across two different systems taking customer orders

Enhanced CMS

capabilities Improve the performance of the system to cater to increasing order volumes

Integrated with various 3rd party systems like Telebiz, MS Dynamics, PIM, Stripe, accessible.

Automation of end to end process with minimal manual intervention

Implementation Success

Website response time has improved with less than 5 sec for critical operations like checkout and less than 2 sec response time for all other journeys.

SEO components required for the migration was successfully addressed on the website launch without which the performance of the website for search would have been affected.

Site developed to work efficiently at 3 breakpoints – desktop, tab & mobile

Upgraded from Adobe Commerce Cloud v2.4.3 to v2.4.5

Benefits

01

Reduced operational cost due to removal of operational redundancy & E2E automation

02

Business is able to self manage content via Page Builder without the intervention of technical support

03

Leveraged multi-tenant platform capabilities of Adobe Commerce by hosting 2 different brands on the same instance.

What specific partner expertise helped the customer to succeed?

1Digitals had Proficient Magento Development team and also clear understanding eCommerce domain
